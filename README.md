If you're wondering how to teach your Ro-Person, you can do all sorts of thing you want but be mindful as teaching them wrong thing will result in a wrong decision that will stress you out in the future and may also be your end/jail. We do not take the responsibility of your actions and things you teach to your Ro-Person, as they are your Personal Robot and we cannot indulge in your own life. But if the Ro-Person is in Ro-Health Center, then we can cure and fix your Ro-Person if you state the condition, Last action, and The Ro-Person's Identity.

# What can i do with this files?
You cannot do something, Modify something, Delete somthing, or replace something on this website files. But after you connect your Ro-Person on the website, you'll get a local part of the website only you and your Ro-Person can see and enter. If you have that, You can costumize anything on that local part imcluding the screen, background, design, etc. You can also download the Web-App itself to actually make the customized design better looking. You can also chat your Ro-Person via the **Chat Your Ro-Person** tab in your Side bar.
