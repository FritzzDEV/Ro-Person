Ro-Person...

A Robot built to have its own personalities and memories... A personal companion that you teach and work with... A Ro-Person that can be your family or friend.

This project aims to make life better and happier by giving individuals a personal companion... One that grows, learns, and evolves through shared experiences!

A Ro-Person is a robot designed to develop its own personality through memories and learnings. Each Ro-Person builds knowledge over time based on what its owner teaches and the experiences it encounters.

All memory is stored internally and cannot be externally modified or replaced. Please follow all rules and guidelines to ensure proper development!

How to Access Statistics?

To fully experience and properly maintain your very own Ro-Person, users must Login Via Ro-Person Website... If you dont know hoe to Login, Please follow the instruction in "Full guide on how to Login and Connect."

Please note that owners must teach and treat their Ro-Person with care and responsibility. The way you guide your Ro-Person will shape its personality and behavior!

Health & Support

All Ro-Person data is private and stored internally within the Ro-Person! Remote repair or modification cannot be done without Ro-Health Help.

If your Ro-Person is damaged, please visit a Ro-Health Center and provide details about the issue and recent interactions with the Ro-Person. If you have any questions abiut Ro-Person, please go to support site.
